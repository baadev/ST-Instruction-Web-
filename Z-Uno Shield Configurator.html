SmartThings installation guide

There's a SmartThings Device Handler installation guide

  1. First you need login to your SmartThings IDE at graph.api.smartthings.com (create a login if you don't have one). Open screenshots

    You may see this form if you recently complete registration and this is your first login

  2. Click on tab "My Device Handlers" Open screenshots

  3. Click on "+ Create New Device Handler" on the top right Open screenshots

  4. Click on "From code" Open screenshots

  5. Go to our repository at github.com and copy code one of Device Handlers Open screenshots

  6. Paste code (Ctrl + V) to text area and click "Create" Open screenshots

  7. Click "Publish" on the top right and after that click "For Me" Open screenshots

  8. Repeat steps 5 - 7 for each of Device Handler from repository

  9. Finnaly you should see all of them in Device Handlers list with Status "Published" Open screenshots

  10. You're done!
